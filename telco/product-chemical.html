Swimming Pool Chemicals

Calcium Hypochlorite Granular Niclon 70G - Japan (45kg/Drum)

Niclon, a brand of calcium hypochlorite, is used around the world in keeping modern life clean. It effectively disinfects and sanitizes by dissolving in water to create a chlorine solution.

Pool Chlorine - Granular HTH 70G - USA (45kg/Drum)

Directions for use: Test pool water and maintain pH between 7.2 – 7.6. Dose your pool with 250g of HTH 70% Granular for every 50,000 liters of water, repeat every day or as needed.

Calcium Hypochlorite STAR-CHLON Japan 70% Chlorine Granules

A highly effective disinfectant for pools, spas, and water supplies. Also used in industries like food processing, textiles, and more.

Calcium Hypochlorite Waterchlon China 70% Granular (40kg Drum)

A high-quality dry chlorinator used for sanitizing pools and industrial water treatment. Available as free-flowing granules with longer shelf-life than liquid sanitizers.

Tablet Chlorine 90% (50kg Drum)

Chlorine tablets are an economical and effective way to disinfect and purify water. Ideal for rural areas or where timely water purification is needed.

Hydrochloric Acid 33%

Used for cleaning brick, concrete etching, and rust removal. This acid is also employed for pH adjustment in swimming pool maintenance and water treatment.
