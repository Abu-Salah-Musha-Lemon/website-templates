Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive education in computer science and engineering, fostering innovation and preparing students to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry. We aim to advance knowledge through cutting-edge research and collaboration with industry leaders.

Vision

We envision being a leading department recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement. We strive to shape the future of technology by cultivating a diverse community of scholars and professionals dedicated to solving complex challenges.

Our History

The Computer Science and Engineering Department was established in 1995 with the goal of providing a top-tier education in computer science. Over the years, the department has grown significantly, adding new programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a diverse group of faculty and students. Our commitment to excellence in research and teaching has positioned us as a leader in the field.

Accreditation

Our programs are accredited by the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology (ABET), ensuring that we meet the highest standards of quality and excellence in education. This accreditation reflects our commitment to providing a rigorous and relevant curriculum that prepares students for success in their careers.

Facilities

Our department boasts modern facilities designed to support a range of research and learning activities. These include advanced computer labs, collaborative workspaces, and specialized research centers focused on areas like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science. Our facilities are equipped with the latest technology to provide students and faculty with the resources they need to succeed.
